CribMatch Zimbabwe – WhatsApp Rental Assistant
=============================================

CribMatch is a simple, WhatsApp-based service that helps people in Zimbabwe find and advertise rental properties quickly and easily.

This website is only here to explain how the service works. All the real action happens on WhatsApp – there are no forms to fill in and no online accounts to create here.

How It Works
------------

- You start a chat with us on WhatsApp  
- You tell us what you are looking for (or what you are renting out)  
- Our WhatsApp assistant guides you step by step

Everything is designed to be fast, mobile-friendly, and easy to use for people in Zimbabwe.

Location
--------

CribMatch is built for the Zimbabwean market, focusing on local rentals and landlords, with communication handled mainly over WhatsApp.

Privacy & Security
------------------

Please do not share your PINs, passwords, or banking details with anyone in WhatsApp or on this website. We will never ask you for confidential banking information.
